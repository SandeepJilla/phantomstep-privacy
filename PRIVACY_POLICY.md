# Privacy Policy for PhantomStep

**Effective Date:** March 28, 2026

## 1. Introduction

PhantomStep ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices.

## 2. Data Collection

### Personal Information
PhantomStep **does NOT collect any personal information** including:
- Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
- Location data
- Personal identifiers
- Health or fitness information

### Usage Data
The App does **not** collect or transmit any analytics, usage data, or device identifiers to external servers.

### Game Data
Game progress, scores, and level data are stored **locally on your device only**. No data is transmitted to external servers.

## 3. Third-Party Services

This App uses no third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

## 4. Data Security

All game data remains on your device. We use standard security practices to protect device data.

## 5. Children's Privacy

PhantomStep is suitable for all ages and does not collect information from children or anyone else.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We encourage you to review it regularly.

## 7. Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact us at:
**Email:** privacy@phantomstep.app
